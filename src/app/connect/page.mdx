# Connect

Client-side SDKs to connect users to web3.

Onboard any user, connect to any wallet, and facilitate any transaction.

<OpenSourceCard title="Connect" href="https://github.com/thirdweb-dev/js" />

## Features

- [Connect:](/connect/connect) Fully customizable Connect Wallet component
- [In-App Wallet:](/connect/in-app-wallet/overview) Email & social login wallets for your customers
- [Account abstraction:](/connect/account-abstraction) Complete toolkit for Account Abstraction
- [Auth:](/connect/auth) Integrate Auth with your app
