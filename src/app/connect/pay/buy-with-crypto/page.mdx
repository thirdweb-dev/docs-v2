# Buy With Crypto

Buy With Crypto handles the complexity of cross-chain routing by leveraging several DeFi liquidity providers to find the most affordable and least complex route for any crypto to crypto transaction.

## Features

- **Free To Integrate:** No upfront costs or subscriptions. Just integrate and unlock crypto purchases for your users.
- **Cross-Chain Execution:** Use funds on any EVM source chain to execute any transaction on a destination chain (e.g. use Polygon funds to execute a transaction on Optimism).
- **Liquidity Aggregation:** thirdweb aggregates several liquidity providers under the hood to ensure that every swap is possible.
- **Collect Fees**: Configure a percentage fee and recipient address for your application to take for each transaction.
- **Built-in Connect Support:** Buy With Crypto is enabled by default for your users through our Connect UI component.
