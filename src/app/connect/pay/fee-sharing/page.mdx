# Fee Sharing

thirdweb shares 30% of our fee through Buy With Crypto with you. To collect fees, all you need to do is set a recipient address.

Your recipient address can be configured in your dashboard by navigating to [Connect > Pay](https://www.thirdweb.com/dashboard/connect/pay) in the side navigation.
